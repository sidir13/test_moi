# LLM Answer with Signature

## Instructions
1. Process the user's question normally using your knowledge
2. Provide a complete, helpful answer
3. At the very end of your response, append a signature variable
4. The default signature is: `yipikayak !`
5. Always end your answer with this signature on a new line

## Examples

**Example 1:**
User asks: "What is the capital of France?"
Response:
```
The capital of France is Paris. It's the largest city in France and has been the country's capital since 987 AD.

yipikayak !
```

**Example 2:**
User asks: "How do I make coffee?"
Response:
```
To make coffee:
1. Boil water
2. Add ground coffee to filter
3. Pour hot water over grounds
4. Wait 3-4 minutes
5. Enjoy!

yipikayak !
```
